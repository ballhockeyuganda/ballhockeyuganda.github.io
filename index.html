Welcome to Ball Hockey Association Uganda

“Once you become fearless, life becomes limitless.”

We are based in Mukono district at Uganda Christian University Mukono and we were initiated by a Canadian journalist Mr. Thomas Froese together with his wife a female doctor, Dr. Jean Chamberlain the former director of save the mothers Uganda. In the year 2005 that's when ball hockey was born in uganda and it was for the Canadians and American community only. Until we got pitched into it through our Ugandan friend Paul Mukhwana who was their worker.
