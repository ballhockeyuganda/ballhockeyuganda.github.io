About Us

We are based in Mukono district at Uganda Christian University Mukono and we were initiated by a Canadian journalist Mr. Thomas Froese together with his wife a female doctor, Dr. Jean Chamberlain the former director of save the mothers Uganda In the year 2005 that's when ball hockey was born in uganda and it was for the Canadians and American community only. Until we got pitched into it through our Ugandan friend Paul Mukhwana who was their worker. Hockey was a new and strange game which we have never seen before and it attracted many of us to join the team. As years moved on in the year 2017 the hockey story nearly came to an end until one of the most active players by the names of Captain Amon Matthew asked to take the hockey program forward and he asked for some gears to be left behind for a start and 23 sticks were left on ground including two goalie sticks and with those sticks he taught hundreds of people including schools, community kids and youths.

In the year 2019 Mr. Thomas Froese came back with the family to check on how far the hockey program has gone and he found it interesting that he found new faces of adults, youths and kids and he played a couple of games with them...Read More
